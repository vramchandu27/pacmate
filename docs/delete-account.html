📱 Delete Account from the App

You can permanently delete your account directly inside the PacMate app. No need to contact us.

  • 1 Open PacMate on your device and make sure you are signed in.
  • 2 Tap your profile avatar on the home screen to open your profile.
  • 3 Scroll down and tap Settings.
  • 4 Tap Delete Account at the bottom of the page.
  • 5 Confirm the deletion. Your account and all associated data will be permanently removed immediately.
⚠️ This action is irreversible. Once deleted, your account and data cannot be recovered. Make sure you have exported or noted any information you wish to keep.

🗂️ What Data Is Deleted or Kept

When you delete your account, here is what happens to each type of data:

Data Type Action Retention Period
Account credentials (email, password) Deleted immediately None
Profile info (name, photo, bio) Deleted immediately None
Trips & budget data Deleted immediately None
Expenses Deleted immediately None
Packing lists Deleted immediately None
Medicine reminders Deleted immediately None
Family links Deleted immediately None
Chat messages Deleted immediately None
Notifications Deleted immediately None
Hidden Gems you submitted Remain public Indefinitely — gems are community content. Your name is removed but the location stays.
Anonymous usage analytics May be retained Up to 14 months (Firebase Analytics standard)

✉️ Can't Access the App? Request by Email

If you are unable to sign in or access the app to delete your account, you can email us and we will manually delete your account and all associated data within 7 business days.

Please include the email address registered with your PacMate account in your request.
